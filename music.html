Anime Music

From anime, we have been given many songs from their openings and closings of the show. Sometimes they are even so well known that even non-
anime watchers know and recognize them. On medias such as TikTok, YouTube, and Spotify there are many people who do "English covers" or
"Nightcore" versions of Japanese songs (Nightcore is essentially a sped up version of the song). It has even gone to the extent of making
games that relate to anime or Japanese music. One example is Osu!, a rhythm game based on clicking and dragging circles in time to the beat.
