Anime Music

From anime, we have been given many songs from their openings and closings of the show. Sometimes they are even so well known that even non-
anime-watchers know and recognize them. Anime/Japanese pop songs have definitely impacted culture all across the world. Especially on media
platforms such as youtube and spotify, there are many people who do "English covers" or "Nightcore" versions of Japanese songs (Nightcore is
essentially a speeded up version of the song). It has even gone to the extent of making games such as ones based on your rhythm skill; an
example of one is Osu!, a rhythm game based on clicking and dragging circles in time to the beat.
