About

This website has been made to inform people who do not know much about Japanese culture/media.
Anime, manga, and Japanese pop music have had an increase in popularity in recent years with
media such as TikTok and YouTube being flooded with anime-related content. Personally, anime
has been a large part of my childhood and daily life. I grew up with shows such as Dragon Ball
and nowadays a large portion of the music I listen to includes Japanese pop or rock music.
