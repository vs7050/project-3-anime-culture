About

This website has been made to inform people who do not know much about a major of Japanese culture that has
influenced a decent portion of the world. Anime, manga, and Japanese pop music have definitely impacted the
modern world in one way or another. In recent times, I have seen an increase of popularity of this part of
the Japanese culture, and wanted to make this website as a little introduction to those who do not know.
