What is Manga?

Manga is the Japanese term for comics. When we hear comics, we tend to think about Charlie Brown or superheroes.
These concepts are usually viewed and enjoyed by males. However, for the Japanese, manga has an appeal to everyone;
there is almost always something for everyone, whether it is child-friendly content, more adult-themed content, or even
slice of life or relatable content. Compared to American comics, the complexity of plots and diversity of genres in
manga outdo them by a mile I feel.
