What is Manga?

Manga is the Japanese term for comics. When we hear comics, we tend to think about Charlie Brown or superhero comics.
These concepts are usually viewed and enjoyed by certain groups of people. However, for the Japanese, manga has an
appeal to everyone. There is almost always something for everyone, whether it is child-friendly content, more
adult-themed content, or even slice of life or relatable content. Compared to American comics, the complexity
of plots and diversity of genres in manga outdo them by a mile.
