What is Anime?

Anime, the japanese way of saying "animation", has become increasingly popular in the western world
as of recent. For reference, Japanese anime is the equivalent to our cartoon shows that we see on TV.
However, it is a little different, with Japanese artists having their own style of drawing. Here is a snippet
of an anime called "That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime":

However, typically, there would not be subtitles if you were in Japan. This version of anime is normally called "sub",
where there are subtitles to go along with the anime. Another version would be called "dub" where the anime is actually
redone by English voice actors.
